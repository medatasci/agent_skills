# Glioma Differential Matrix

Status: final evidence-count target met; needs human expert review.

| Rank | Differential / MR-RATE Contrast | Why It Is Confused | Supports Glioma | Supports Alternative | Research Handling |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Brain metastasis | Enhancing mass and edema overlap. | Infiltrative FLAIR, callosal spread, single dominant lesion without systemic cancer. | Multiple gray-white junction lesions, known cancer. | Adjudicate solitary lesions. |
| 2 | Abscess | Ring enhancement overlaps. | Elevated perfusion/infiltration, no central marked restriction. | Central diffusion restriction and infection context. | Exclude infection-like lesions. |
| 3 | Lymphoma | Enhancing mass with restriction. | Necrotic infiltrative glioma pattern. | Deep/periventricular homogeneous restriction. | Use DWI and clinical context. |
| 4 | Tumefactive demyelination | FLAIR and enhancement overlap. | Progressive infiltrative tumor, perfusion elevation. | Open ring, MS-like lesions, less mass effect. | Adjudicate. |
| 5 | Subacute infarct | Enhancement and FLAIR overlap. | Nonvascular infiltrative mass. | Vascular-territory DWI/evolution. | Require temporal/vascular review. |
| 6 | Radiation necrosis/pseudoprogression | Treated glioma enlarges/enhances. | Serial progression/high perfusion. | Treatment timing/low perfusion/stabilization. | Separate endpoint. |
| 7 | Meningioma | Mass and edema overlap. | Intra-axial infiltrative tumor. | Extra-axial dural base/hyperostosis. | Exclude dural masses. |
| 8 | Cavernous malformation | Hemorrhagic lesion overlaps. | Infiltrative enhancing/nonenhancing tumor. | Popcorn lesion/hemosiderin rim/DVA. | Separate vascular malformation. |
| 9 | Gliosis/encephalomalacia | Chronic FLAIR abnormality overlaps. | Expansile progressive lesion. | Volume loss/stable chronic injury. | Use prior imaging. |
| 10 | Cerebral edema | Edema can dominate report. | Tumor focus with infiltrative pattern. | Edema from infarct, PRES, trauma, infection. | Use as covariate, not label alone. |
