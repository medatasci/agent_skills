# Schwannoma Differential Matrix

Status: final evidence-count target met; needs human expert review.

| Rank | Differential / MR-RATE Contrast | Why It Is Confused | Supports Schwannoma | Supports Alternative | Research Handling |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Intracranial meningioma | CPA/IAC enhancing mass overlap. | Nerve-centered IAC mass, IAC widening, ice-cream-cone shape. | Broad dural base, dural tail, hyperostosis. | Adjudicate CPA/IAC masses. |
| 2 | Epidermoid cyst | CPA lesion with mass effect. | Enhancing nerve sheath tumor. | DWI-bright nonenhancing insinuating lesion. | Use DWI. |
| 3 | Arachnoid cyst | CPA CSF-like mass. | Enhancing solid mass. | CSF intensity, no enhancement, no restriction. | Exclude benign cyst. |
| 4 | CPA/IAC metastasis | Enhancing IAC/CPA lesion. | Slow course, typical nerve-centered mass. | Cancer history, rapid symptoms, other metastases, DCE/DWI pattern. | Require cancer/adjudication context. |
| 5 | Facial/trigeminal schwannoma | All are schwannomas but different nerve origin. | Lesion follows CN VIII when vestibular. | Facial canal/Meckel cave/jugular foramen centered. | Preserve location subtype. |
| 6 | Paraganglioma | Jugular foramen mass. | Nerve sheath lesion with less vascular flow void pattern. | Salt-and-pepper, vascularity, jugular bulb origin. | Use CT/angiographic context. |
| 7 | Lipoma | IAC/CPA lesion. | Enhancing mass. | Fat signal, T1 bright, fat suppression. | Exclude fat lesions. |
| 8 | Glioma/brainstem tumor | Brainstem compression vs invasion. | Extra-axial CPA/IAC mass compressing brainstem. | Intra-axial infiltrative brainstem lesion. | Use compartment signs. |
| 9 | Hydrocephalus/ventriculomegaly | Large VS can cause hydrocephalus. | Identified CPA mass causing obstruction. | Primary ventricular enlargement without tumor. | Treat hydrocephalus as complication. |
| 10 | Posttreatment change | Treated tumor can enlarge. | Sustained growth beyond pseudoprogression window. | Transient enlargement/central hypoenhancement after SRS. | Require treatment date and serial MRI. |
